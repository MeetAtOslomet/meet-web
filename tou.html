The application will give information such as name, age, language interest and qualifications at OsloMet.The hobbies of user are also shown which helps to find a correct match. All the students and staff at OsloMet who are registered in the app can use the app and communicate with their matches. A matching algorithm is used to find the correct match based on their language interest and hobbies. The institution carries no responsibilities for misuse of this application and the communication and information exchanged between the user and their match. How much information is shared must be controlled by the user themselves and focus should be made in learning and exchanging knowledge about language they want to learn.
